VBA & Excel Macros

Keywords: Data analytics, VBA, Macros

In the business world, we encounter a lot of data that has different relevance to us at different times. Sometimes there's so much data that opening source files and parsing through it is an intimidating task.

While working with excel, I dabbled with macros in order to shorten the data-parsing process and make investigation a little less onerous.
